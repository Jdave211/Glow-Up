# GlowUp Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

GlowUp ("GlowUp," "we," "us," or "our") provides a mobile app and related services for personalized beauty and looksmaxxing recommendations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, retain, and protect personal information.

By using GlowUp, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- The GlowUp mobile app.
- Related websites, APIs, and support communications.
- Data collected directly by us and by service providers acting on our behalf.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

- Account and contact information: name, email address, account identifiers.
- Profile inputs: skin type, goals, concerns, routine preferences, and onboarding responses.
- Chat and routine data: prompts, responses, saved routines, progress notes, timeline entries.
- Purchase/subscription data: subscription status and transaction metadata from Apple (we do not receive full payment card numbers).
- Device and usage data: app version, device model and OS, identifiers, log data, crash and performance diagnostics, and interaction events.
- Support data: information you provide when contacting support.

### Face Data and Photo Analysis Data

If you choose to use GlowUp photo analysis or photo check-ins, we collect:

- Photos you upload for analysis, including front-face, left-side, right-side, and optional scalp photos.
- Derived face/skin analysis outputs from those photos, including detected skin type and tone, hydration score, oiliness score, texture score, detected concerns, redness areas, pore visibility, confidence values, and recommendation tags.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

- Provide and operate GlowUp features.
- Analyze uploaded photos and profile inputs to generate personalized recommendations.
- Personalize routines, product suggestions, and progress tracking.
- Process and manage subscriptions and entitlement access.
- Send service messages, reminders, and push notifications (if enabled).
- Detect abuse, fraud, and security incidents.
- Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve product quality and reliability.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

### Planned Uses of Face Data

GlowUp uses collected face data only to:

1. Generate personalized skin analysis results.
2. Build and refresh personalized routines and recommendations.
3. Support optional progress comparisons over time if photo check-ins are enabled.
4. Display private progress insights within the same user's account.

## 4. AI Processing and Consent

GlowUp uses AI systems to analyze text and images and generate recommendations.

- GlowUp asks for user permission before sending face photos, face-analysis inputs, or chat data to third-party AI processing.
- If a user does not grant this permission, the app does not send that data for those AI features.
- We do not sell personal information.
- We do not use user photos for advertising targeting.

## 5. How We Share Information

We may disclose information to:

- Service providers for hosting, storage, AI inference, notifications, analytics, customer support, and security.
- Platform providers such as Apple (for App Store services, Sign in with Apple, and in-app purchases).
- Legal and compliance recipients when required by law.
- Corporate transaction parties in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets.

We require processors to handle personal data with appropriate confidentiality and security.

### Face Data Sharing, Third Parties, and Storage

GlowUp does not share face data with other users.

Face photos and related analysis data are shared only with service providers needed to run the feature:

- **Supabase:** stores private application data and private photo files for GlowUp.
- **OpenAI API:** processes AI analysis and recommendation requests sent by GlowUp.

Primary storage location for GlowUp face data is private Supabase database and storage infrastructure controlled by GlowUp.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy.

- Account/profile/routine/progress data: retained while your account is active, unless earlier deletion is requested and legally permitted.
- Chat content: retained to provide chat history and app functionality unless deleted.
- Backups/logs: retained for limited operational and security periods, then deleted or anonymized.
- Legal retention: we may retain limited records where required by law or for dispute/security handling.

### Face Data Retention and Deletion

GlowUp does **not** retain face data indefinitely.

- If Photo Check-ins is enabled, uploaded face photos and derived face-analysis payloads are retained for up to **90 days** to support progress tracking.
- If Photo Check-ins is disabled, raw uploaded onboarding photos are deleted after analysis completes.
- Face-photo references and retained face-analysis payloads beyond the retention window are automatically redacted by backend retention jobs.

Third-party retention related to face data:

- **Supabase:** stores uploaded face photos and derived face-analysis data on GlowUp's behalf for the periods described above (up to 90 days when Photo Check-ins is enabled, or shorter when disabled) so GlowUp can provide analysis results and optional progress history.
- **OpenAI API:** API content is not used to train OpenAI models by default and may be retained for up to **30 days** for abuse and misuse monitoring.

When you request account deletion, we delete or de-identify personal data within a reasonable period, subject to legal exceptions.
